Playlists

Playlists in halo_radio are all managed automaticly via the DBSync utility that should be running in cron ;-). Here is a list of the playlists and what they contain:

    Playlists
  • Master - all songs in the mp3 archive are added to this playlist. This is the default playlist.
  • Requested - all songs that have been requested one or more times.
  • Auto-AI - songs that have a positive popularity.
  • Announce - songs existing in the announce_path. This is a special list, created for announcements. mp3's in this direcly will be randomly chosen after announce_delay seconds, and forcefully played before the next request or random song selection.
